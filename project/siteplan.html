My Site Plan

Site Name: besys.tech

This name represents my startup tech company in the Philippines focused on creating beautiful and useful websites for the community.

Site Purpose:

This site aims to provide my visitors with website ideas through the many projects/portfolios owned by me so they can leverage them for their own business websites.

Scenarios

  • What kind of websites will customers want to build for their own small businesses?
  • Where can I find contact information for the web developer for a freelancing job?

Color Scheme:

I will use carmine: #931621ff dark-slate-gray: #28464bff as my primary and secondary colors.

Typography:

I will use Roboto, Arial, sans-serif and Poppins, sans-serif as my primary and secondary fonts.
